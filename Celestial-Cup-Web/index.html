# Celestial Cup

Overview and Vision Statement

Celestial Cup is a thrilling football game where universes compete for the ultimate trophy. Players aim to outscore their opponent within a 2-minute match, with a 5-goal limit. The game combines nostalgic gameplay inspired by Sports Heads Football with a dramatic storyline akin to Dragon Ball Super's Tournament of Power.

Gameplay

Players compete in Player vs Player mode on a dynamic football pitch. The controls are straightforward, allowing for an engaging experience:

  • Player 1: W (Jump), A (Left), D (Right), B (Kick), V (Dash)
  • Player 2: up arrow (Jump), left arrow (Left), right arrow (Right), P (Kick), O (Dash)

The first player to score 5 goals or achieve the highest score within the time limit wins!

Story

In a tournament between universes, two champions—Mero of Universe Phaelys and Toli of Universe Edir—fight for glory and the coveted Celestial Cup. The fate of their universes rests on their skills.

Game World

The game takes place on a football pitch set against a vibrant, cosmic background. Players control their characters within boundaries, ensuring intense, action-packed matches.

Player Goals

The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent. Each player starts on opposite sides of the pitch, racing for the ball as the countdown ends.
